Scrambled Eggs on Toast

Description

This is a unique culmination of eggs taken to the pan seconds after being surfaced from the chicken, cooked to perfection, then spread on a base of crunchy albeit delicate pieces of bread, previously spread with silky butter.

It is a complex and precise procedure, but once you pull it off, the time and dedication invested are truly worthwhile.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat the pan to a medium heat
  2. Crack your eggs(4) into the pan
  3. Cook the eggs slowly
  4. Toast your bread while the eggs are cooking
  5. Once toasted, butter toast
  6. Once the eggs are soft and smooth and not overcooked, immerse them with the bread
